How it works
1
Install it like an app — it's web-based, so just open the link and add it to your home screen. No App Store needed.
2
Play your song through Spotify, YouTube Music, whatever you use — Mic Drop runs over the top of it.
3
Sing. The app listens through your microphone, tracks your pitch in real time, and matches it against the song.
4
Get your verdict. Then share the receipt. Studio recordings work best — live versions can trip it up.
